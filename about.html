About me

I am currently in my senior year as a graphic design student at Eastern Washington University. Some of my favorite designers include Alphons Mucha, Paul Rand and Briar Mark. My favorite photographers include Ansel Adams and Thomas Barbey. I hope to work in advertising and become a pilot in the National Guard once I graduate.

In my free time, I enjoy traveling (especially flying,) photography, fishing, video games, swing dancing and singing in my car. I also enjoy weightlifting, running and Spartan Races.
